    Background of Zorin OS
    >Who started Zorin OS? The story of Zorin OS began in the summer of 2008 when Artyom Zorin and his brother Kyrill tried Linux for the first time.
    Inspirations The brothers were astonished at how much better it was than Windows and macOS and wondered why everyone wasn’t using it. However, when they showed Linux to their father, they realized that Linux was difficult to use for new users, and the desktop was confusing.
    What they tried to do ⇝ The two brothers had a mission to create a Linux distribution with a user-friendly interface. However, the only problem was that they were only 12 and 14 years old, and making their own distribution seemed impossible.

    Issues Faced Along the Way (Environmental/Real Life)
    There weren’t any computer science curriculum at their school, so the brothers learned software development by reading books, watching tutorials on YouTube, and Googling. Nine months later - on 1 July 2009 - Zorin OS 1.0 was released. After graduating from school and studying Computer Science and Business in university at Trinity College Dublin, Artyom and Kyrill are working on Zorin OS full time.

    What makes Zorin OS Special?
    Zorin objective was to make it as effortless as possible to transition from Windows or macOS to Linux. If you’ve used Windows or macOS, you will be able to use Zorin OS with ease
    Summary You have a fully functional Linux distribution behind of all the desktop refinements and quality of life improvements!
