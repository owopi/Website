    Elementary OS

    What is Elementary linux Elementary OS is yet another free and open-source Linux distribution based on Ubuntu.
    Uniqueness? It distinguishes itself from other distributions due to its custom desktop environment called Pantheon and its many custom apps, including Photos, Music, Videos, Calendar, Terminal, Files, etc.; all built by the few full-time Elementary OS developers.
    Gui When looking at linux distrubutions such as Arch Linux, Ubuntu and others you would not think that there are aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly gui to guide you however elementary os is one of the unique operating system that does this justice! Just like zorin os, Elementary OS has the beautiful aesthetics and GUI that comes with it.
    Developing Elementary OS is one of the only Linux distributions with a built-in app store with paid apps focused on indie developers. However, Elementary OS claims that “each app has been reviewed and curated by elementary to ensure a native, privacy-respecting, and secure experience.”
